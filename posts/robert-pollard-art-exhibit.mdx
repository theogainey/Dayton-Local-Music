Throughout the month of December, [Skelton Dust Records](https://www.facebook.com/SkeletonDustRecords) will be host to a special art exhibit. This exhibit will feature works by Dayton native and [Guided By Voices](https://www.facebook.com/GuidedByVoicesOfficial) frontman Robert Pollard. Beginning December 1st, the exhibit will be open weekly from Tuesday through Sunday. In addition to the month-long run, a celebration with the artist will be held on December 13th.

Not only a talented musician and songwriter but also a talented visual artist, Robert Pollard began making collages as a child. Since then many Guided By Voices releases have featured his collages as cover art. Additionally, his collages have gone on to be featured in a number of high profile exhibits. Most notably displaying at Michael Imperioli's Studio Dante in New York and the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame in Cleveland.

## More Information
Skelton Dust Record is located at 133 E. 3rd St.Dayton, Ohio. This exhibit will run the entire month of December between the hours of 11-7 pm Tuesday-Saturday and Sunday 12-6 pm. The Celebration with the artist will take place on December 13th, 7- 9 pm
